    General questions:

    How may I schedule my appointment?

    You may schedule your appointment through the website, Facebook(zoyas_nails_), Instagram(@zoyas_nails_), or by calling-texting: 331-318-2891 and via email: zoyasnails@gmail.com

    What if I need to reschedule/cancel my appointment?

    If you need to reschedule/cancel your appointment, please let us know via text or phone 24 hours before your scheduled appointment. This allows us enough time to fill your appointment and give other clients an opportunity to get their services done. Please read carefully {new link}.

    What if I am running late or need to do last minute cancelation?

    No-show, same day cancellation, or nighttime cancellations for morning appointments (morning appointments include 8 through 11AM) will be charged a full price of the service. Nail art may be limited if you are running late. If you are running over 15 minutes late to your appointment, you will be asked to reschedule, and will be charged a cancellation fee.

    What if the regular salon hours doesn't work for me?

    We love our clients and value your time and needs. If you want to schedule your appointment before or after regular salon hours please feel free to contact us with your requests. 30$ extra will be added to the service fee and the earliest appointment is 6a.m. and the last appointment is 8 p.m.

    Should I reply to the appointment reminder?

    We can't wait you to confirm your appointment! When we send you curtesy appointment reminders: it is required you to confirm with us at least 24 hours before your scheduled time, otherwise your appointment will be automatically cancelled.

    Where can I park?

    There is metered street parking in front of the salon ( 1 hour max from 9a.m.-6p.Mon-Fr). There is also free parking available on N Nothingam Ave or N Neva ave. (a short walk to the salon). Please No Parking behind the Salon.

    Can I bring my family members to the appointment?

    Please do not bring friends, family, children, pets. This is your selfcare time, so let's use it to relax and unwind. Less distractions means more productivity!

    What payment methods do you accept?

    Our payment options include:Cash, Venmo App, Cash App. (We unfortunately dont accept personal checks,Pay Pal, Bank of America, Chase Quick Pay, Zelle). There is no ATM machine near the salon, so be sure one of this payment options works for you. If you are paying cash please bring extra in case your total comes out to more than originally planned - due to nail art, special requests, etc.

    What if I want to change the type of service?

    If you have any appointment/service changes, let me know in advance. For example: If you've booked mani and pedi, and you choose to get done only one of them, unfortunately, I will need to charge you for both. We book our schedule based of appointment and time required for each service, so if there is a change, we need to adjust and accomodate all our clients.

    Do you do extensions or acrylic nails?

    Unfortunately, we dont do any type of extensions(hard gel, acrylic). We work only with natural length.

    Do you have Gift Cards?

    Yes, you may purchase Gift Card Here or by phone: 331-318-2891.
